Our Story

Count501 began, when Janis was looking for a new software development project. Since he plays darts for a hobby, the idea of a new dart counter came to his mind. After thinking about the idea and talking to his friends about the project, Nino and Patrick jumped in.

Our journey has been marked by constant innovation, rigorous testing with ambitioned players, and a commitment to enhancing the game without changing its essence. Today, we're proud to offer solutions that are trusted by casual players and professionals alike.
